Hi, this is Xiangyu Xiao

My name is Xiangyu Xiao, an UX Designer from China. I am currently a Master’s student majoring in User Experience Design at Loughborough University. I have obtained a BA degree in Product Design at Southeast University.

My research interest focuses on creating meaningful tangible interactions with products using advanced technology.

My design philosophy is rooted in user-centered and utilitarian principles, incorporating user needs and preferences into the design process of user-product interactions, creating experiences that are more in line with user needs and sustainable.
